## Design Context

### Users
Families with children (primarily parents) booking entertainment venues in Saudi Arabia. Users book activities for kids, birthday parties, and family outings. Context: mobile-first, often while on-the-go or planning leisure time.

### Brand Personality
**Voice:** Playful yet trustworthy, family-focused, Middle Eastern hospitality.
**Tone:** Exciting and energetic - the app should feel like the anticipation before fun.
**3-word personality:** Vibrant, Family-First, Joyful

### Aesthetic Direction
The existing red/orange/gold palette aligns with the energetic, premium feel. Warm gradients convey excitement and celebration (birthday party vibes). Gold accents suggest premium experiences without being cold or corporate. Continue with this direction but refine toward more intentional, less "AI-generic" aesthetics.

### Design Principles
1. **Energy through color, not clutter** - Use gradients purposefully for CTAs and key moments, not decoration
2. **Family-friendly typography** - Readable, approachable, warm (keep MontserratArabic as it already supports Arabic)
3. **Playful interactions** - Micro-animations that feel bouncy and fun without being childish
4. **Trust signals** - Clear booking confirmations, easy cancellation, visible pricing
5. **Mobile-first density** - Information-rich but breathable; thumb-friendly targets
